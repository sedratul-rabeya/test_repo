The name 'Nakshi kantha'became popular after the poet Jashimuddin's poem 'Nakhshi khnatar math'was published in 1929.Traditional are made for family use.Old or new cloth and thread are used to make these quilts. Mymenshingh,jamalpur, rajsahi,fridpur ,Bogura and jashar are most famous for this craft Now it is produced commercially.
